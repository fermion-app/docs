The Partial payment dashboard provides a comprehensive view of your installment payment collections. It helps you track students on payment plans, monitor collection trends, and identify overdue payments at a glance.

## Accessing the Partial payment dashboard

Follow these steps to access the Partial payment dashboard:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open instructor dashboard">
    After logging in to your school, navigate to your **Instructor dashboard**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to billing & earnings">
    In the sidebar menu, scroll down to the **Billing and earnings** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click on earnings">
    Select **Earnings** to open your earnings dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Partial payments tab">
    Click on the **Partial payments** tab to view the partial payment dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Summary overview

The top section of the Partial payment dashboard displays key metrics and collection information:

### Total students

Shows the total number of students enrolled in partial payment plans, with a breakdown of their payment status:

- **Active**: Students with upcoming installments
- **Completed**: Students who have finished all their payments
- **Overdue**: Students with missed payment deadlines

Example: `9 total students: 5 active, 1 completed, 3 overdue`

### Collection in range

Displays the total amount collected within a selected date range. This helps you track revenue from partial payments over specific periods.

- **Amount**: The total collection amount (e.g., `₹31,175`)
- **Date range**: The period for which collections are calculated (e.g., `Nov 4 - Dec 4, 2025`)

### Chart date range

Allows you to select a custom date range for viewing collection trends. Use the calendar icon to adjust the period shown in the Collections Overview chart.

## Collections overview chart

The Collections overview chart provides a visual representation of your partial payment collections over time.

**Purpose**: Track partial payment collections over time

The chart displays:

- **Y-axis (Amount Collected)**: Shows the collection amount, typically ranging from ₹0 to ₹10,000 with increments
- **X-axis (Date)**: Displays the timeline with date labels
- **Line Graph**: A green line with shaded area below showing the amount collected on each date

## Partial payment students table

The Partial payment students table provides detailed information about each student enrolled in an installment payment plan.

![](https://67d6ad5ef66ee0901532fd2f.storage.fermion.app/public-uncached/object-store-public-files/uploads/04-12-2025/Arc_Rk1ovzsfuL.mkgmve.png)

### Table Columns

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Student** | The name and profile of the student enrolled in the payment plan |
| **Product** | The course, bundle, or digital product purchased through installments |
| **Progress** | Shows the payment progress (e.g., "1 of 3 payments") with a visual progress bar |
| **Next Installment** | The amount and due date of the next payment (e.g., "₹5,000 Due Oct 15, 2025") |
| **Status** | Current payment status: **Active**, **Overdue**, or **Completed** |

### Payment status types

- **Active** (Blue badge): Student has upcoming installments that are not yet due
- **Overdue** (Red badge): Student has missed a payment deadline
- **Completed** (Green badge): Student has successfully completed all installments

<Warning>
Students with **Overdue** status require immediate attention. Consider sending payment reminders or reaching out to help them complete their payments.
</Warning>

### Understanding progress

The progress indicator shows:
- **Current payment number**: Which installment the student is on (e.g., "1 of 3")
- **Visual progress bar**: A blue bar that fills based on completion percentage
- **Total installments**: The total number of payments in the plan
