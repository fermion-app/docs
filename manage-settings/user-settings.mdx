Your instructor account settings allow you to manage how you appear across your courses and on your website. Keeping your profile up-to-date helps students recognize you easily and builds credibility. In this section, you can update your name, profile photo, tagline, long description, and email. Each change ensures that your presence is consistent and professional throughout the platform.

## Access account settings

You can access the account settings with the help of the following steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access account settings">
    To start updating your account, go to your **Instructor Dashboard** sidebar and click **Settings â†’ Account Settings**. This will open your account management page where all editable fields are displayed.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Update profile photo and name">
    Your **display name** and **profile photo** are key elements of your identity on the platform.  
    - **Your Name:** Update the name that will appear on your course pages and website. Use your full name or the name you prefer students to see.  
    - **Profile Photo:** Click **Change** to upload a high-quality square photo. This photo will appear on all your course pages and is important for recognition and branding.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update tagline and long description">
  ![](https://67d6ad5ef66ee0901532fd2f.storage.fermion.app/public-uncached/object-store-public-files/uploads/24-10-2025/bL6HEp8iFd.nkxlvh.png)
    Your **tagline** and **long description** help students understand who you are and what you teach.  
    - **Tagline:** Add a short, descriptive tagline that summarizes your teaching style or expertise. This will appear on your website.  
    - **Long Description:** Provide a detailed description of yourself, your experience, and the courses you teach. You can format this text using Markdown for better readability.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update email">
    Your email is crucial for notifications and account security. To ensure your email address is correctly updated, you will need assistance from our support team.  
    - To change your email, contact **support@codedamn.com**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Update password

Passwords protect your account and all associated course content. Updating your password regularly is a good security practice, especially if you suspect your account credentials may be compromised. This section allows you to securely set a new password while keeping your account protected.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access password settings">
    Scroll down to the **Update password** section within **Account Settings**. This is separate from your profile information to emphasize security.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set a new password">
  ![](https://67d6ad5ef66ee0901532fd2f.storage.fermion.app/public-uncached/object-store-public-files/uploads/24-10-2025/chrome_afFnMbUELi.fuofea.png)
    - Enter your **New password** in the first field.  
    - Confirm your new password in the **Confirm new password** field to avoid typing errors.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save changes">
    After entering your new password, click **Update password** to apply the changes. Make sure your new password is strong and not used elsewhere for better security.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Now your account details are updated across your school!
