The Email Log displays a list of all outbound emails sent from your school, including notifications, announcements, invitations, and system messages. You can use this log to review delivery status, recipients, subject lines, and timestamps for each email.

To access the Email Log, go to **Settings â†’ Email Log**. Here, you can:

- View list of all emails sent from your school
- Filter emails by recipient email or subject.
- See details such as subject, recipient, and time sent

This feature helps you monitor communication, troubleshoot delivery issues, and keep track of important messages sent to your users.
