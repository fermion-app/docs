This document lists some of the commonly asked FAQs related to setting product pricing and managing courses on Fermion.

<Accordion title="How many currencies can I support inside my course?">
You can support your course in 180+ countries with 100+ currencies available for your pricing plan.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do I need to integrate my own payment gateway on the platform?">
No, you do not need to integrate your own payment gateway. Fermion handles and manages all payments and payouts for you. For additional support, [reach out to us](mailto:vishnupriya@fermion.app).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What payment gateways will be made available for my course?">
We use Razorpay, PhonePe, and our in-house UPI solution for domestic payments (UPI, credit/debit cards, netbanking). For international purchases, gateways like Stripe and PayPal are available. Fermionâ€™s in-house UPI solution reduces transaction costs to 0.5% compared to 2% on platforms like Razorpay.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can international users see their currencies in my course?">
As long as you have supported their country's currency in your pricing plan, they can see it and purchase your product. You can also enable default fallback currencies. 
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I set up EMI based plans for my course?">
Yes, you can build and set up custom EMI plans for your course. Learn more about setting EMIs on Fermion [here](/setup-pricing/setup-emi).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do EMIs need to be configured with any bank?">
No, EMIs do not need to be configured with any bank. Fermion handles and manages EMIs in-house.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How will students be notified of their next EMI payment?">
Students receive timely reminders via email before each payment is due. If a payment is missed, access to the product is restricted.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What are custom checkout fields and how can I use them?">
Custom checkout fields allow you to collect additional information from students during checkout. You can create text input or dropdown fields to capture data like graduation status, course preferences, or other relevant details. Learn more [here](/setup-pricing/checkout-fields).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How can I share my course with a direct checkout link?">
Direct checkout links allow you to share a course or a pricing plan with students directly, bypassing the landing page. This is useful for emails and campaigns. Learn more [here](/custom-landing-pages/checkout-page-direct-links).
</Accordion>
