An **Iframe Embed** allows you to embed external content from any website directly into your course. This feature enables you to integrate third-party tools, interactive content, or any web-based resources that complement your course material.

## How do I add an iframe embed?

<Steps>

<Step title="Expand course items">
Click the <code>+</code> button to expand the course items' view.<br/>
<img src="https://67d6ad5ef66ee0901532fd2f.storage.fermion.app/public-uncached/object-store-public-files/uploads/01-12-2025/Arc_Qpjao7D1gl.dosjxw.png" alt="expand-course-items" />
</Step>

<Step title="Add an IFrame Embed item">
Click on <code>IFrame Embed</code>. This will add an iframe embed item inside your chosen section.<br/>
<img src="https://67d6ad5ef66ee0901532fd2f.storage.fermion.app/public-uncached/object-store-public-files/uploads/01-12-2025/Arc_zWOzsEh3nU.bxzaah.png" alt="add-iframe-embed-item" />
</Step>

<Step title="Add title and URL">
After adding, set a title and enter the URL you want to embed.<br/>
<img src="https://67d6ad5ef66ee0901532fd2f.storage.fermion.app/public-uncached/object-store-public-files/uploads/01-12-2025/Arc_v1ypm1GiJc.ljogfr.png" alt="add-title-url" />
</Step>

<Step title="(Optional) Use experimental YouTube player">
If you are embedding a YouTube video, you can choose to use an experimental player for enhanced experience. This custom player removes all the YouTube branding and the redirects, recommendations and cards that are usually at the end of any YouTube video.<br/>
<img src="https://67d6ad5ef66ee0901532fd2f.storage.fermion.app/public-uncached/object-store-public-files/uploads/01-12-2025/Slack_GJp7fQMcAf.nelsjp.png" alt="experimental-youtube-player" />
</Step>

</Steps>
