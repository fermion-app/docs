**Resources** are downloadable files and materials that you can provide to your students as part of your course. Resources can include PDFs, documents, code files, templates, or any other downloadable content that supports student learning.

## How do I add resources?

<Steps>

<Step title="Expand course items">
Click the <code>+</code> button to expand the course items' view.<br/>
<img src="https://67d6ad5ef66ee0901532fd2f.storage.fermion.app/public-uncached/object-store-public-files/uploads/01-12-2025/Arc_Qpjao7D1gl.dosjxw.png" alt="resources1" />
</Step>

<Step title="Add a Resources item">
Click on <code>Resources</code>. This will add a resources item inside your section.<br/>
<img src="https://67d6ad5ef66ee0901532fd2f.storage.fermion.app/public-uncached/object-store-public-files/uploads/01-12-2025/Arc_sv8NDd2dma.mjamkb.png" alt="resources2" />
</Step>

<Step title="Enter details">
Enter a <code>Title</code> and <code>Label</code> for your resource.<br/>
<img src="https://67d6ad5ef66ee0901532fd2f.storage.fermion.app/public-uncached/object-store-public-files/uploads/01-12-2025/Arc_UfEEvCQzZu.pqtkvf.png" alt="resources3" />
</Step>

<Step title="Edit and upload files">
Click on the newly added <code>Resource</code> item to edit it and add files.<br/>
<img src="https://67d6ad5ef66ee0901532fd2f.storage.fermion.app/public-uncached/object-store-public-files/uploads/01-12-2025/Arc_urCzT5FwVG.digtpr.png" alt="resources4" /><br/>
From here, you can edit the Name, Label and also upload files:<br/>
<img src="https://67d6ad5ef66ee0901532fd2f.storage.fermion.app/public-uncached/object-store-public-files/uploads/01-12-2025/Arc_tgfGl4sFGc.cepbqt.png" alt="resources42" />
</Step>

<Step title="Save and publish">
Save your changes and publish the item to make it available to students.
</Step>

</Steps>
