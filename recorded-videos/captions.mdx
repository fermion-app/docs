sidebarTitle: 'Captions'
icon: closed-captioning
---

<Steps>

<Step title="Go to Video Library">
Navigate to **Dashboard → Recorded Videos → Video Library** to view all your uploaded recordings.
</Step>

<Step title="Open Video Settings">
Find the video you want to caption and click the **Settings (⋮)** icon next to it.
![](https://67d6ad5ef66ee0901532fd2f.storage.fermion.app/public-uncached/object-store-public-files/uploads/03-11-2025/hMABELzS1l.ikepqt.png)
</Step>

<Step title="Select AI Transcription">
In the video settings panel, toggle the **enable AI transcription for this video** option to automatically generate captions in the same language used within the video.
![](https://67d6ad5ef66ee0901532fd2f.storage.fermion.app/public-uncached/object-store-public-files/uploads/03-11-2025/chrome_Gk19ptvP7t.vofrry.png)
</Step>

<Step title="Wait for Processing">
Fermion will begin transcribing your video automatically.  
The processing time depends on the video length.
</Step>

</Steps>


---

### Global Settings for Captions

You can also enable this feature globally for all recorded videos uploaded to your school.

<Steps>

<Step title="Open School Settings">
Navigate to **Dashboard → Settings → School Settings → Recorded Video Settings**.
![global-captions-toggle](https://67d6ad5ef66ee0901532fd2f.storage.fermion.app/public-uncached/object-store-public-files/uploads/01-10-2025/Arc_LNO75xKmlS.hpzxjm.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Enable Global AI Transcription">
Toggle **Enable AI Transcripts** to automatically generate captions for every video uploaded under your school.
</Step>

</Steps>
