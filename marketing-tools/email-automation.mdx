Fermion provides an end-to-end ecosystem for all of your marketing tools, this includes various email automation flow like:

1. A payment confirmation email is sent to users immediately after they make the purchase to your course.
2. An email is dispatched, along with course access, whenever someone purchases your course.
3. An email is dispatch one day, as well as 30mins prior to your live classes.

These are just three of the many email flows that are already linked to your account. We ensure maximum student participation inside your course, via these email flows.

If you have a specific email flow in your mind, useful for your use-case, feel free to share with us by reaching us at [support@codedamn.com](mailto:support@codedamn.com).

## Setting up a custom email sender

Fermion provides an option to send emails from a dedicated address under your sub-domain.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add a new domain">
    Go to **Settings > Email Senders** and add a new domain to send emails from.  
    ![](https://67d6ad5ef66ee0901532fd2f.storage.fermion.app/public-uncached/object-store-public-files/uploads/11-09-2025/email-sender-step-1.rjbpjm.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access DNS records">
    Click on the newly created sub-domain to access the DNS records required to connect Fermion with your sub-domain.  
    ![](https://67d6ad5ef66ee0901532fd2f.storage.fermion.app/public-uncached/object-store-public-files/uploads/11-09-2025/comet_j7sDUtxa5w.rzjtgc.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add DNS records to your host">
    Copy and paste the provided DNS records into the DNS section of your hosting provider.  
    ![](https://67d6ad5ef66ee0901532fd2f.storage.fermion.app/public-uncached/object-store-public-files/uploads/11-09-2025/comet_ufp6vyNmxd.dspbag.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify records">
    Once the records are verified, the status will update to indicate verification, and your custom email sender will be ready to use.
  </Step>
</Steps>
