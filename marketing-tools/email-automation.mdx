Fermion provides an end-to-end ecosystem for all of your marketing tools, this includes various email automation flow like:

1. A payment confirmation email is sent to users immediately after they make the purchase to your course.
2. An email is dispatched, along with course access, whenever someone purchases your course.
3. An email is dispatch one day, as well as 30mins prior to your live classes.

These are just three of the many email flows that are already linked to your account. We ensure maximum student participation inside your course, via these email flows.

If you have a specific email flow in your mind, useful for your use-case, feel free to share with us by reaching us at [support@codedamn.com](mailto:support@codedamn.com).

## Setting up a custom email sender
Fermion provides an option for you to send emails from a dedicated address under your sub-domain.

- Step 1: Go to Settings > Email senders and add a new domain to send emails from.
![email-sender-step-1](https://67d6ad5ef66ee0901532fd2f.storage.fermion.app/public-uncached/object-store-public-files/uploads/11-09-2025/email-sender-step-1.rjbpjm.png)
- Step 2: Click on the newly created sub-domain to access the DNS records to connect Fermion with your sub-domain
![email-sender-step-2](https://67d6ad5ef66ee0901532fd2f.storage.fermion.app/public-uncached/object-store-public-files/uploads/11-09-2025/comet_j7sDUtxa5w.rzjtgc.png)
- Step 3: Copy and paste these DNS records in the DNS section of your hosting provider
![email-sender-step-3](https://67d6ad5ef66ee0901532fd2f.storage.fermion.app/public-uncached/object-store-public-files/uploads/11-09-2025/comet_ufp6vyNmxd.dspbag.png)
- Step 4: Once the records are verified , the state changes to indicate the same and your custom email senders are set up.
