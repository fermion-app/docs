## Documentation & troubleshooting

All of Fermion’s functionalities, including detailed guides and troubleshooting tips, 
are comprehensively documented at [docs.fermion.app](https://docs.fermion.app). 
This should be your primary reference whenever you need clarification or step-by-step instructions.  

For example, if you face playback issues with recorded videos, the [Video Troubleshooting](https://docs.fermion.app/recorded-videos/troubleshooting-playback) 
section can help resolve them quickly.  

Stay up to date with platform improvements and product changes through the [Changelog](https://docs.fermion.app/changelog).
