---
title: 'eBooks Inside Courses'
description: 'Learn how to connect your eBook to a course and allow enrolled students to access it from within the course.'
icon: layer-group
---

You can enhance your **[course](/setting-up-course/prerequisites)** experience by linking an eBook to a course. This allows students enrolled in the course to access the eBook automatically without needing a separate purchase or enrollment.

<Steps>
    <Step title="Open the Course Builder">
    - Go to your **Instructor Dashboard → Courses → Open Course Builder**.  
    - Select the course where you want to link your eBook.

    ![Open Course builder](https://67d6ad5ef66ee0901532fd2f.storage.fermion.app/public-uncached/object-store-public-files/uploads/06-10-2025/course%20builder%20open.pdcqad.png)  
    </Step>

    <Step title="Navigate to Syllabus Tab">
    - In the course builder, go to the **Syllabus** tab.  
    - Identify the section where you want to add the eBook.  
    </Step>

    <Step title="Add eBook to Section">
    - Click **Add in this section** for the chosen section.
    ![](https://67d6ad5ef66ee0901532fd2f.storage.fermion.app/public-uncached/object-store-public-files/uploads/07-10-2025/add%20in%20this%20section.zgbelu.png)  
    - Select the **eBook** option in **Add course item** modal.
    ![](https://67d6ad5ef66ee0901532fd2f.storage.fermion.app/public-uncached/object-store-public-files/uploads/07-10-2025/add%20course%20item.becmtx.png)
    </Step>

    <Step title="Upload or Link eBook Directly">
    You can upload a **new eBook** or link a **preexisting one** directly from the **Select eBook to add** section.

    To link an existing eBook: Choose one from the list and click **Link with course item**.

    ![](https://67d6ad5ef66ee0901532fd2f.storage.fermion.app/public-uncached/object-store-public-files/uploads/27-10-2025/43EFQP78Mr.zlohgn.png)
    
    To upload a new eBook: Click **Quick Create eBook**, enter the eBook title, and upload your PDF file.  
        Once uploaded, click **Create Ebook** to make it available for linking. This eBook will be created in an unlisted state.
    ![](https://67d6ad5ef66ee0901532fd2f.storage.fermion.app/public-uncached/object-store-public-files/uploads/27-10-2025/V9mUphr1fh.hrghua.png)

</Step>

    <Step title="Access eBook Inside the Course">
    Once linked:  
    - Students enrolled in the course can open the eBook directly from the course dashboard.  
    - Any updates to the eBook (like new versions) are reflected automatically for all students.
    </Step>
</Steps>


<Info>
Linking eBooks to courses ensures a smooth experience for students and helps you bundle content efficiently. To enroll specific students outside of a course, refer to [Enroll Students](/digital-products/enroll-students).
</Info>
