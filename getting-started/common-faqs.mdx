## General Questions

### Do I need technical knowledge to use Fermion?
No, Fermion is designed to be user-friendly and requires no coding knowledge for basic operations. However, advanced features like custom CSS styling and API integrations are available for those who want more control.

### What types of content can I create on Fermion?
You can create:
- Live classes with up to 10,000 participants
- Recorded video courses with DRM protection
- Interactive coding labs (HTML/CSS, React, Node, Python, Java, Rust)
- IO coding labs (SQL and other languages)
- Ebooks
- Community spaces
- Custom landing pages

## Live Classes

### How many students can attend my live class?
Fermion supports up to 10,000+ students in a single live session with smooth, uninterrupted streaming.

### Can I invite co-instructors or guests to my live class?
Yes, you can invite up to 50 participants to the stage during a live session. They can share their screen, turn on their camera, and use their microphone.

### Is there a time limit for live classes?
No, there is no restriction on the duration of your live classes. You can teach for 6+ hours in a single session.

### Are live classes automatically recorded?
Yes, once your live session ends, Fermion automatically converts it into a video recording and makes it available to your students. You can also enable AI-generated captions in the same language used during the stream.

### What video quality is supported for live streaming?
Fermion supports streaming up to 1080p resolution for your live classes.

## Video Content & Security

### How is my video content protected?
Fermion provides built-in DRM (Digital Rights Management) support to protect your content from piracy and unauthorized sharing. You can also enable video watermarking to identify anyone sharing or pirating your content.

### Can I add captions to my recorded videos?
Yes, Fermion offers AI-generated captions for recorded videos. You can enable this feature when uploading or processing your videos.

### Can I embed videos outside of Fermion?
Yes, you can embed both live sessions and recorded videos on external websites using Fermion's white-labeled embedding options.

## Coding Labs

### What programming languages are supported in coding labs?
Fermion supports interactive coding labs for:
- HTML/CSS
- JavaScript/Node.js
- React
- Python
- Java
- Rust
- SQL (IO labs)

### What are the differences between Interactive and IO coding labs?
- **Interactive labs:** Provide a full development environment in the browser with live preview (ideal for web development)
- **IO labs:** Focus on input/output testing, perfect for algorithmic problems and SQL queries

## Payments & Pricing

### How do I receive payments from my students?
Fermion integrates with payment gateways to process student payments. You can manage your payouts from the instructor dashboard under payout settings.

### How long does it take for payouts to be credited?
Fermion settles payouts within 24 hours from our end. Banks typically take an additional 3-4 business days to credit the amount to your account.


### Can I create custom pricing for my courses?
Yes, you have full control over your course pricing. You can also set up affiliate marketing programs and offer discounts.
